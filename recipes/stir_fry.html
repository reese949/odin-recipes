Chicken & Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

For the stir-fry:

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or sesame oil)
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned or sliced into thin matchsticks
  • 1 small zucchini, sliced into half-moons
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons green onions, chopped (for garnish)

For the sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce (use low-sodium for a healthier version)
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce (or hoisin sauce for a sweeter flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar (or lime juice for a tangy kick)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or brown sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (to thicken sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes or sriracha (optional, for heat)

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce:

    In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, honey (or sugar), sesame oil, chili flakes (if using), and cornstarch-water mixture. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables:

    Slice the vegetables as noted in the ingredients list (bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, onion, broccoli) and set aside.

  3. Cook the Chicken:

    Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable or sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken to the pan in a single layer. Let it cook undisturbed for 2-3 minutes until it starts to brown on one side. Then stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Stir-Fry the Vegetables:

    In the same pan, add another tablespoon of oil if needed. Add the garlic, ginger, and onion to the pan. Stir-fry for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the remaining vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, zucchini, and broccoli) and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes, until they are tender-crisp. You can cover the pan briefly to steam the broccoli if needed.

  5. Combine Chicken & Sauce:

    Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the sauce over the stir-fry and toss everything to combine. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and coat the chicken and vegetables. If the sauce gets too thick, add a splash of water to loosen it up.

  6. Serve:

    Garnish with chopped green onions and serve over steamed rice or noodles. You can also sprinkle some sesame seeds on top for added texture.

Tips:

  • Protein Options: You can swap the chicken for beef, shrimp, tofu, or even tempeh, depending on what you prefer.
  • Veggies: This stir-fry is very versatile. You can use other veggies like snap peas, mushrooms, baby corn, or bok choy.
  • Heat Level: Adjust the amount of chili flakes or sriracha to your spice tolerance.
  • Make it gluten-free: Use tamari or coconut aminos instead of soy sauce and ensure that your oyster sauce is gluten-free.
